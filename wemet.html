Women in Mechanical Engineering Tea

Virginia Tech
Wednesdays, 12:00-1:00pm
Goodwin Hall, Room 443


Description

Women in Mechanical Engineering Tea (WeMET) provides an opportunity for graduate students across different research groups to get to know each other, socialize, discuss the highs and lows of graduate school, and foster diversity. WeMET aims to support members of the Virginia Tech ME community who identify as women, trans, non-binary, and gender nonconforming, and we welcome people of all genders.

Contact

For more information, contact Amanda Wei (amwei4@vt.edu) or Dylan Losey (losey@vt.edu)

Description

Women in Mechanical Engineering Tea (WeMET) provides an opportunity for Virginia Tech graduate students across different research groups to get to know each other, socialize, discuss the highs and lows of graduate school, and foster diversity. WeMET aims to support members of the Virginia Tech ME community who identify as women, trans, non-binary, or gender nonconforming, and we welcome participants of all genders.
